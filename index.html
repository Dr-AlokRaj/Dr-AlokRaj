Research Focus

Eco-sensitivity Assessment

Monitoring and assessment of the Aravalli Range using geospatial approaches under changing climatic conditions with 15 distinct environmental indicators.

Forest Fire Modeling

Development of Forest Fire Spread Models using Satellite Remote Sensing and Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) in Sikkim Himalayas.

Water Resource Management

Decision Support System for Cleaning Ganga and water resource management using advanced geospatial technologies.

Air Quality Assessment

Spatial and temporal variability analysis of air pollutants over sub-humid climatic conditions and their health impact assessment.

Land Degradation Studies

Land degradation neutrality (LDN) assessment using pressure-state-response models and MODIS data products for sustainable land management.

Remote Sensing Applications

Advanced remote sensing techniques for environmental monitoring, forest health assessment, and sustainable development goal implementations.
